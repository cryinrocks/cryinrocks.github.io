About the Show

This site is mostly a result of me breaking my arm and the boredom that came with it.

I started DJing at WCBN in the fall of 2019, and started my regular show in January 2020.

I wasn't really sure what it was going to be, or what to call it, but after a long night of working on an EECS project, and my partner saying the words, "Y Against X," I thought that it might be a cool name.

I'm sure I could come up with some deep explanation of how it connects to the show, but there really isn't anything there

But that's about it, hope you enjoy
